About our Solar System :


The Solar System is a Colony of 8 Planets and 1 Star. The planets comprise of Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune.
Earlier, there were nine planets, including pluto, but in 2006 it was given the title of a "Dwarf Planet". The Earth is the only planet that is inhabitable. Planets like
Mercury and Venus are too hot for life to survive, other planets are too cold. The inner solar system comprises Sun, Mercury, Venus, Earth, and Mars. Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus,
and Neptune form the outer solar system. These four planets are massive in size; hence they are called Giant Planets. Each planet revolves around the sun in its own orbits at its own speed.
